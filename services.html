Welcome to OldCare Services

AAt Old Care, we believe in providing a safe, compassionate, and nurturing environment for seniors to live comfortably and with dignity. Our services are designed to cater to the physical, emotional, and social needs of each resident, ensuring they feel valued, respected, and supported every step of the way. From personalized healthcare and wellness programs to engaging activities and social gatherings, we aim to make every day enriching and enjoyable. Our dedicated team is here to assist with daily tasks, medical needs, and companionship, so our residents can focus on what matters most: enjoying life to the fullest. Explore our range of services below to see how we bring warmth and care into each aspect of senior living.

Further Support And Activities

House Keeping And Laundry Service

Regular cleaning, laundry, and linen changes to maintain a clean, comfortable, and hygienic environment for residents.

Medication Management

Professional oversight and administration of medications, ensuring correct dosages and adherence to schedules, which is crucial for residents with complex medication needs.

Transportation Assistance

Scheduled transportation for medical appointments, social outings, and other essential errands, helping residents stay connected with the outside community.

Fitness and Wellness Programs

Activities like yoga, tai chi, light exercise classes, and walking clubs that promote physical health, strength, and flexibility while catering to individual mobility levels.

Memory Care Services

Specialized care for residents with dementia, Alzheimer’s, or other memory-related conditions, including cognitive therapies and secure, safe living areas.

Spiritual and Religious Support

Opportunities for religious services, spiritual counseling, and visits from clergy members for residents who wish to maintain their spiritual practices and beliefs.
